A music app with two sides. One for discovery, one for play.

SocialSound is the iOS / iPadOS / tvOS / macOS companion to your music library. News, concerts, and setlists for the artists you follow — plus seven multiplayer games for the next time you're with friends.

One app, two sides

On first launch, you pick a side. Switch any time from Settings. Each side has its own theme, its own tabs, its own use case.

Pulse · Discovery

News, releases, shows.

A library-aware companion: every artist in your Apple Music or Spotify library, organized into one feed of what they're doing right now.

  • Daily artist news from 20+ music publications
  • Upcoming concerts filtered by your location
  • New release tracking with calendar reminders
  • Recent setlists from artists on tour
  • Cross-platform link sharing — convert any music link to Apple, Spotify, or YouTube Music in one tap
Play · Party games

Music night, anywhere.

Seven multiplayer music games for road trips, holiday gatherings, the bar, the line at Disney. Host on a TV, on your iPad, on a Mac — or pass-and-play on a single phone.

  • Music Bingo · Album Cover Reveal · Mystery DJ
  • Name That TV Theme · Music Video Guessing
  • Team Music Quiz · Mixtape Trivia
  • Per-game leaderboards across every session
  • 30-day rolling Mixtape — every song from every game you played, ready to add to your library

Seven games. One stack.

Every game uses the same connect-and-play infrastructure. Connect once, switch games whenever the room calls for something different.

▦

Music Bingo

5×5 cards, 30-second clips, first to five-in-a-row wins. Pick a theme; the host plays the soundtrack.

◐

Album Cover Reveal

Cover slowly comes into focus on the host's screen. Easy: pick from 4 chips. Hard: type the title. Speed bonus.

♪

Mystery DJ

Each round, one player's library secretly DJs. Guess whose track is playing — and how well you actually know your friends.

📺

Name That TV Theme

30-second clip from a famous TV intro. Pick the show from the chips. Sitcoms, crime dramas, decades — your choice.

▶

Music Video Guessing

Host plays a music video. Easy / medium-muted / hard-with-three-strikes. First correct chip wins the round.

⚐

Team Music Quiz

Buzz in on your phone, scoreboard on the TV. Four round types: Name the Song, Finish the Lyric, Decade Decoder, Name the Artist.

♫

Mixtape Trivia

Each player submits 3 artists. Random album cover + 30 seconds of a track. Guess the artist (1pt), album (2pt), and song (3pt).
