Last updated: 2026-05-09

Impostor Software LLC (“we,” “us”) participates in affiliate-marketing programs. This page explains how those relationships work and what they mean for you, the user, in compliance with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission’s Endorsement Guides .

What this means

When you tap a link in our SocialSound iOS / iPadOS / tvOS / macOS app or on this website that takes you to a partner store (for example, a sticker shop, an online merch retailer, or a concert-ticket marketplace), we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase. This commission comes from the partner—the price you pay is unchanged.

Programs we participate in

We participate in or are evaluating the following affiliate programs. This list is updated as new programs are added or removed:

  • Etsy Affiliate Program (managed via Awin / Rakuten)
  • Ticketmaster Affiliate Program (managed via the impact.com network)

How we choose what to surface

Items shown in the app or on this site are chosen because we believe they’re relevant to the artists you follow or the games you play. Affiliate relationships do not influence which artists, news sources, or games we feature—only the storefront / merchandise surface.

Your data

Tapping an affiliate link opens the partner’s site in your device’s default browser. Once you leave our app or website, the partner’s privacy policy applies. We don’t share personal information with partners; the network identifies us only by an opaque publisher ID embedded in the link.

For details on what data SocialSound itself processes, see our Privacy Policy.
